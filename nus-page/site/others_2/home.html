About Learning and Vision Lab (LVLab)

The Learning and Vision Laboratory is a leading research group focused on advancing the fields of computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to develop innovative algorithms and systems that enable machines to understand, interpret, and interact with the visual world. With a strong emphasis on both theoretical and applied research, we collaborate with industry partners and academic institutions to address real-world challenges and push the boundaries of AI technology.

Our research spans a wide range of topics, including efficient AI, multimodal learning like reasoning, agent, and generative models. We are committed to fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment where students, researchers, and faculty can thrive and make impactful contributions to the field.
