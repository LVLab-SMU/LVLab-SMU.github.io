History of LV Lab

The learning and Vision Lab (LV Lab) was founded by Shuicheng Yan in 2007. Under his leadership, the lab flourished until 2012, when Shuicheng Yan departed to join 360 Company.

Taking the reins in 2012, Jiashi Feng continued to advance the lab's mission until 2021, when he embarked on a new journey at ByteDance.

In 2021, Xinchao Wang assumed leadership of LV Lab, steering it until 2025 when he established the xML Lab .

In a significant development in 2025, Shuicheng Yan returned to National University of Singapore to once again lead the learning and Vision Lab, marking a new chapter of innovation and growth.
