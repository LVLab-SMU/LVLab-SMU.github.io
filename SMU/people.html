Director
Dr. Pan Zhou
Assistant Professor,
School of Computing
and Information Systems,
Singapore Management University

Dr. Pan Zhou is tenure-track Assistant Professor at the School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University. Previously, he was a Senior Research Scientist at Sea AI Lab and a Research Scientist at Salesforce. He received his Ph.D. from the National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Jiashi Feng and Prof. Shuicheng Yan, and graduated at Peking University, working with Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Chao Zhang. He also collaborated closely with Prof. Xiaotong Yuan. In addition, he spent several enriching months as a visiting student at Georgia Tech in 2018 and at Princeton University in 2019.
