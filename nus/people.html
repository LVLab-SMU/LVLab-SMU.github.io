Director
Dr. Shuicheng Yan
Distinguished Professor(Practice),
School of Computing,
National University of Singapore

Prof. Shuicheng Yan is a Fellow of the SAEng, AAAI, ACM, IEEE, and IAPR. His current research centers on e-AGI: efficient, executive, and evolving. Consistently recognised among the World's Highly Cited Researchers (10 years, H-index >150), Prof. Yan started at NUS in 2007/12, later served as Group Chief Scientist at Sea Group and other industry roles, and returned to NUS in 2025/01. His teams have secured multiple top distinctions at Pascal VOC and ImageNet (ILSVRC), and notably achieved a "grand slam" at ACM Multimedia with three Best Paper Awards, two Best Student Paper Awards, and one Best Demo Award.
